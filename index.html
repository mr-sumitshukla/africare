Our Story

Founded on the principles of compassion, integrity, and holistic healing, AfriCare Hospital has been a beacon of healthcare excellence in Nigeria. Our unique approach in modern medicine, ensuring comprehensive care that addresses both the body and the spirit.

We offer a comprehensive range of healthcare services, including General Medicine, Emergency care, Surgery, Orthopaedics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology specialized treatments of IVF.

Wellness & Physiotherapy Rehabilitation

We offer integrated wellness services in Ayurveda (Ancient Indian Herbal medicine) which is known for its holistic approach to health, emphasizing natural healing through herbal remedies, diet, yoga, meditation & lifestyle changes to balance the body, mind & soul.

Blending holistic therapies with evidence based physical recovery techniques to enhance well-being, restore mobility, and foster long term health for individuals seeking natural and effective healing.

Oncology Excellence

In Oncology, we aim to deliver the best, most effective & affordable cancer treatments to Nigeria, making them accessible & inexpensive for the average person.

Our expert team prioritizes quality, safety, and patient-centred care, ensuring personalized treatment plans that promote long-term wellness, faster recovery, and a healthier future for all.

Meet our dedicated medical professionals committed to providing exceptional healthcare

DR. JOBIN JOY

Medical Director

MD General Medicine, Fellowship In Endocrinology

Certified Medical Tourism Facilitator, Air Ambulance Doctor

Brings 12 years of distinguished medical expertise to our hospital. With eight years of service in Nigeria, including urban Lagos and rural Benin state, he has a proven track record in establishing and managing clinics and hospitals for leading industries.

Certified as a Medical Tourism Facilitator and Air Ambulance Doctor, Dr. Joy's versatility shines through. During the COVID-19 pandemic, his unwavering dedication saved countless lives within the Indian community in Nigeria, later in India, earning him recognition from both Indian and Nigerian communities for his selfless service during the lockdown.

Driven by a deep sense of gratitude, he is committed to giving back to his fatherland, Nigeria, through his dream of establishing a hospital to serve his fellow Nigerians. Having been blessed by the land's richness, this venture is his way of honouring its legacy while drawing inspiration from motherland, India.
